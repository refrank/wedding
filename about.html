Gifts?

Sometimes when people go to a wedding they feel they should bring a gift. We really appreciate how much effort and expense it takes on your part to come. It would be such a gift to us for you to be here! Getting us a gift is definitely not expected.

Wedding Gown

Hey! Rebecca here. I want to let y'all in on something: my wedding gown isn't white. Some have asked me why that is. I knew I wouldn't have the luxury of one-on-one chats about this with everyone we're inviting, yet some of you might be curious when you see me.

I've long been one to play around with personal style. When I was a toddler I renounced frilly, lace socks. When I was in elementary school I wore one ladybug romper for weeks on end. I've been through phases of wearing all black, all rainbow, and having an unusual affinity for bowling shoes. (I know.)

I had no assumptions of what I would wear for my wedding and had fun considering everything from a glittery pant suit to going barefoot to making my own outfit from scratch. When it comes down to it, there are several reasons that wearing white isn't something I feel a personal connection to. If anyone wants to talk more about this with me, reach out and I'll be more than happy to chat!

I'm sure some folks are doing the whole wink, wink, nudge, nudge “kids these days...” sigh. Just wanted to give y'all a heads up so nobody faints when you see me wearing a cheerful, bright blue dress ;)
